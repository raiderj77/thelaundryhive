Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to facilitate laundry services, including:

  • Account Data: Name, email, and password for Business Owners.
  • Service Data: Customer addresses, phone numbers, and laundry preferences to enable delivery logistics.
  • Payment Data: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store full credit card numbers.

2. How Data is Shared

We share Customer data with the specific "Laundry Partner" (Laundromat) chosen by the user. We do not sell data to third-party advertisers.

3. Data Retention

Order history is retained for as long as the Business Owner maintains an active subscription to provide analytics and customer support.
